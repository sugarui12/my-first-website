C³X / CCC Design Lab

Self-taught programmer & UX/UI explorer. Japanese philology grad blending code, culture, and cars into human-centered design stories.✧•˚

Hi, I’m Jolly Rouge — a Japanese philology graduate, self-taught programmer, and UX/UI explorer. Fluent in seven languages, I’m fascinated by how culture, psychology, and design influence the way we experience the world.

This curiosity drives my main focus in frontend development and UX/UI design, where I blend user experience, cultural insight, and code to create digital spaces that feel intuitive, alive, and deeply human.

Cars inspire me as a side hobby —not as machines, but as moving designs that reveal how form and function shape our experience.

I created the C³X vision — Code, Culture, and Cars, with UX as the bridge. It’s not about merging them into one, but exploring them side by side through a shared lens: how human psychology leaves its mark on design. A website, a language, a car —different forms, all created by the human mind.

Welcome to my CCC Design Lab!
